May 20 1996: This was the last day of the Ultima Online pre-alpha test. The character art took a dramatic change for the better once beta started many months later. Blackthorn and British surveyed the crowd while a half dozen or so summoned orcs made mincemeat. One player was asking in perfect roleplaying manner "Why doth thou kill us my lord!" So began the historic tradition of killing as many players as possible during official gaming events.
